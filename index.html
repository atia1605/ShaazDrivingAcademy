About Us

Shaaz Driving Academy is a Ministry Approved Beginner Driver Education course provider. All of our in-class and in-car curriculum is taught by professional, fully licensed instructors. We are dedicated to providing quality driver education and have served the GTA for over 30 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

On your final day of in-class instruction, you will receive an in-car instructor's name and number. You will then contact the instructor and book appointments based on availability.

Log on to www.drivetest.ca, fill in your information and book your road test for the next available date at a DriveTest centre near you.

In-class can be done without the G1 license. G1 license is required for in-car instruction.

Balance of payment can be paid by cash or cheque at our driving school location.
